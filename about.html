ABOUT OUR LITTLE PACK

Why we made this website

This little space on the internet is dedicated to our three beloved dogs. It helps us share their personalities, photos, and important information in case they ever get lost.

They are family

Our dogs are not just pets. They are part of our family and a huge part of our daily life. They make our home happier, louder, funnier, and more alive.

Why the QR code matters

If one of them ever gets lost, someone can scan the QR code on their collar and be redirected to their page. That page can show their name, photos, and our contact details so they can be returned home safely.

A cute digital scrapbook

This website is also our fun way of showing them off. From sleepy photos to chaotic moments, this site is a small scrapbook of the dogs we love.

Made for the good boys

With their different colors and personalities, each one has their own charm. This site lets each dog have a page that feels special and easy to recognize.
